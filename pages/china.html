Chine

During my 2 years of work in China as a graphic designer, I got lost in a few places in China, between Yangshuo, the Longchen reefs, Xitang and many other places.

This first photo shows a general view of the artistic center in which I worked. An enormous potential for development and life, which unfortunately was wasted in the opening of another artistic center some 500m further down.
